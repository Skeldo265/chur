Welcome to Our Church

Vision Statement

“To be a Christ-centered, Spirit-empowered church transforming lives and communities through love, discipleship, and service.”

Mission Statement

“To preach the Gospel, nurture believers through sound teaching, build strong fellowship, and reach out to the community with compassion and the love of Christ.”

Church Goals

Spiritual Goals

  • To win souls and lead more people into a personal relationship with Jesus Christ.
  • To disciple believers for spiritual maturity and strong Christian character.
  • To promote consistent prayer, worship, and Bible-centered living.

Community Goals

  • To serve the physical, emotional, and spiritual needs of the community.
  • To carry out outreach programs for vulnerable groups and families.
  • To build strong partnerships that promote peace, unity, and development.

Church Development Goals

  • To strengthen leadership through training and mentorship.
  • To grow church membership and encourage active participation in ministries.
  • To expand church facilities and resources to support ministry growth.
